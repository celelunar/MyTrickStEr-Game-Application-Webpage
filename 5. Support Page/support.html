System Requirements

Make sure your PC at least fulfill the minimum requirements to launch the Game.

Minimum Requirements

Storage 10 GB
RAM 4 GB
OS Windows 10
GPU Intel HD 4000 or Radeon Vega 8
CPU Intel Core i5-4570 or Ryzen 3 2200G

Recommended Requirements

Storage 10 GB
RAM 8 GB
OS Windows 10
GPU NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or Radeon RX 460
CPU Intel Core i7-4790K or Ryzen 3 3200G
