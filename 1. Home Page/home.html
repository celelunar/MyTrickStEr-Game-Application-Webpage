Welcome, Soul Divers!

A hero caught in between two worlds, Will you have what it takes to save both?

Embark on epic quest to unlock the mysteries of The Nameless Kingdom, adopt a familiar and test your skills!

Introduction

You are about to enter a brand new world as a beta tester of a virtual reality game called Soul Divers. But once you explore and become stronger, you'll realize this world is not a virtual one. A mysterious girl called Rania entrusts you with a mission: Rebuild your kingdom ransacked by powerful foes and save the two connected worlds from certain destruction!

World Map

A captivating landscape, a mesmerizing tapestry of magical realms awaiting exploration. From ancient forests hiding secrets, to otherworldly vast deserts. Each region is meticulously crafted with vibrant colors and whimsical designs which create this seamless interweave between ethereal plains and bustling towns, providing a visually stunning backdrop for the epic adventure. This living, breathing canvas enhances the immersive experience, inviting soul divers to uncover the mysteries, engage in epic battles, and immerse themselves in the enchanting narrative that unfolds.

Towns

See the stark difference between bustling and peaceful cities. Though, they're just as mesmerizing to one another.

Eastern Heartlands

A mysterious land studded with ancient ruins. Go for a run with Catrumpus through this peaceful landscape where unexpected advantures lurks within.

Burning Desert

A vast desert in the southern Summerlands. If you get lost in the desert, just look for the giant airship in the sky. A brilliant golden city awaits you beneath.

Witch's Woods

A mystical forest shrouded in never-ending night. Don't get distracted by the enthralling moonlight, you might get bewitched!

Winter Wonderland

A realm of snow and ice, home of the yetis. It's bitter cold, but you'll feel warm when you meet new friends.
